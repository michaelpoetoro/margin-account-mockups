BRD Formulas Reference
Equity = Cash Balance + Unrealized PnL
Total Initial Margin = Σ (Notional × IM Rate)
Total Maintenance Margin = Σ (Notional × MM Rate)
Remaining Margin = Equity − Total Initial Margin
Margin Health Ratio = Equity / Total Maintenance Margin

Health Bands:
Green MHR ≥ 1.5 — Healthy
Yellow MHR ≥ 1.2 — Warning
Red MHR < 1.2 — Close-Only
Liquidation MHR ≤ 1.0 — Auto-Liquidate

Rates: IM = 50%, MM = 50%
